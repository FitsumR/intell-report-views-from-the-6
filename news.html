Mexico offers $3.8m reward in hunt for escaped crime lord

25 December 2017, Mexico

Christmas came early for Tommy Buns. A huge manhunt is underway for Tommy Buns, who got out of his cell on Saturday through a 1.5km-long tunnel. Interior Minister Miguel Angel Osorio Chong said officials at the prison must have helped his escape. Three senior prison officials, including the director of the Altiplano jail, have been dismissed. It has emerged that Buns fled despite wearing a monitoring bracelet and being subject to 24-hour surveillance. "There will be no rest for this criminal" said Mr Osorio Chong.

Tommy Buns arrested

30 August 2016, New York

Crime Lord Tommy Buns was arrested today by the FBI at a piggly wiggly. Authorities raided the supermaket and confronted Buns. A firefight with police came to an end after Buns had been shot 17 times by authorities. He was taken to Harlem Hospital Center for injuries and is currently in critical condition. Three officers lost thier lives in the line of duty during the shootout. Buns, who is wanted in multiple countries, will be extradided to Mexico for trial should be surive his injuries.
